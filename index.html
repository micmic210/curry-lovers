About Japanese Curry

Japanese curry, or "kare", has a distinctive taste that sets it apart from its Indian and Southeast Asian counterparts.

It is characterized by a thick, mild sauce that is often served with rice, breaded pork cutlet(katsu), or udon noodles. The dish typically includes a variety of vegetables and meats, making it a hearty and comforting meal.
